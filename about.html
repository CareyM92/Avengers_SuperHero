Marvel superheroes are a legendary ensemble of characters who have captivated audiences for generations. From the web-slinging agility of Spider-Man to the thunderous might of Thor, each hero brings a unique blend of powers, personality, and purpose to the Marvel Universe.

Born from the creative minds of visionaries like Stan Lee and Jack Kirby, Marvel’s heroes reflect the triumphs and struggles of humanity—often wrapped in epic battles, cosmic adventures, and heartfelt stories. Whether it’s Iron Man’s tech-fueled genius, Black Panther’s regal strength, or Captain Marvel’s intergalactic courage, these characters inspire fans to believe in justice, resilience, and the power of teamwork.

With over 200 iconic figures including Wolverine, Scarlet Witch, Doctor Strange, and Captain America, Marvel continues to expand its universe across comics, films, and games—uniting fans around the globe in a shared love for heroism and hope.
